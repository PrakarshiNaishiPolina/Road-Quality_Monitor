# 🛣️ Road Quality Monitoring System 🚦

Welcome to the **Road Quality Monitoring System**, an intelligent solution designed to monitor and assess road conditions using advanced technologies. The system aims to identify road damages such as potholes, cracks, and uneven surfaces, enabling timely maintenance and improving road safety.

---

## 💡 About the Project

The **Road Quality Monitoring System** is a smart application developed to automate the process of detecting and analyzing road conditions. Traditional road inspection methods are often manual, time-consuming, and expensive. This project leverages modern technologies to continuously monitor road quality and provide accurate insights for maintenance authorities.

By analyzing road images and sensor data, the system helps in early detection of road defects, reducing accidents and improving transportation infrastructure.

## ✨ Features

* 📷 **Road Image Upload** – Upload road images for analysis.
* 🔍 **Defect Detection** – Detect potholes, cracks, and damaged road surfaces.
* 📊 **Road Quality Assessment** – Analyze and classify road conditions.
* ⚠️ **Damage Severity Classification** – Categorize road damage based on severity.
* 📈 **Analytics Dashboard** – Visualize road quality statistics and trends.
* 💾 **Data Storage** – Store road inspection records for future analysis.
* 🎨 **User-Friendly Interface** – Clean and responsive design.

---

## 🚀 How It Works

1. Upload a road image or capture it using a camera.
2. The system preprocesses the image.
3. Computer Vision techniques analyze the road surface.
4. Defects such as potholes and cracks are detected.
5. The road condition is classified based on severity.
6. Results are displayed along with visual analytics.
